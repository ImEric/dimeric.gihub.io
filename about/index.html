About Me

Jan 24, 2016

Hi! My name is Eric Hu.

I am currently a UCLA Student majoring in Mathematics of Computation.

I was born in Yueyang, a small city located in the centeral part of China. I spent my first 15 years there until I received a govenment scholarship to continue my high school in Singapore in 2009. I spent my most meaningful four years in Singapore, where I learned proper English and met my life long friends. In 2013, I took a gap year off and finally decided to take my journey further to the United States. From 2014 onwards, I have been staying in Los Angeles, California.

I like painting a lot and I’ve always been regreting not taking Art as my major. Nonetheles, I am still a big fan of programming, simply for the joy of exploration and creation. I am learning iOS development right now and you may take a look at some of my side projects on github.
